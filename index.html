面试 miànshì • Job Interview
面试 miànshì

面试官问了我很多问题。

The interviewer asked me many questions.

"面试" (miànshì) means to interview or to have an interview. It can be used as both a noun and a verb. In HSK 3 context, it usually refers to job interviews.
