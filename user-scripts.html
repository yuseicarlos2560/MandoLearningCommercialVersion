Lesson 57 | Script

Discuss returning home after studying abroad

Focus on emotional vocabulary and cultural nuances of returning to one's roots.

家
shì a , nǐ jiù shuō lái wǒmen gōngsī miànshì de zhè jǐ gè ba ......
是啊，你就说来我们公司面试的这几个吧……
"Yeah, just take these few people who came to our company for interviews as an example..."
wǒ juédé tāmen dōu hěn yǒuyòng , dànshì nǐ de jīngyàn zuì fēngfù.
我觉得他们都很有用，但是你的经验最丰富。
"I think they are all very useful, but your experience is the most extensive."
huíguó zhīhòu , wǒ xiǎng yào gèng duō de shíjiān péi fùmǔ.
回国之后，我想要更多的时间陪父母。
"After returning home, I want more time to accompany my parents."
